About Nzimase Logistics

Founded in 2026, Nzimase Logistics (Pty) Ltd is a proudly 100% black-owned South African transport and logistics company, offering reliable and cost-effective road freight solutions across South Africa and the SADC region. Our team of experienced logisticians ensures that every delivery is safe, on time, and efficient. From raw materials to finished goods and specialized cargo, we provide flexible transport solutions tailored to our clients’ needs. With a strong network of trusted carriers and a modern fleet, we help businesses reduce costs, improve supply chain performance, and gain a competitive edge.

Driving Change, Giving Back

At Nzimase Logistics, we believe that true success goes hand in hand with giving back. While we are a young company, our vision reaches far beyond transport and logistics—we are committed to uplifting the communities we serve. We proudly support initiatives that provide care, shelter, and opportunities for those in need, partnering with local organisations to make a real, tangible difference. Whether it’s supporting education, shelter, or community development programs, we aim to sow seeds of hope, dignity, and growth. As we grow, so does our impact. Every mile we drive, every partnership we forge, and every project we support is a step toward building a stronger, more empowered community. At Nzimase Logistics, giving back is not just an initiative—it’s part of who we are
